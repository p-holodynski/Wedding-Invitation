Dress Code

Dear guests,

For our big day on this beautiful island, we have chosen a style that reflects our love for elegance and simplicity, while keeping the warm weather in mind. We invite you to adopt a classic and relaxed bohemian dress code:

For the ladies: We suggest wearing long, elegant dresses made of light and breathable fabrics. Solid colors are preferred. Feel free to add a bohemian touch with delicate jewelry. And remember, floral patterns are for curtains, not dresses!

For the gentlemen: A light shirt with a suit or chic trousers is perfect. You can opt for neutral or pastel colors. For a relaxed touch, consider accessories like suspenders or a hat. And if you’re torn between a tie and a bow tie, choose the one that makes you feel like James Bond on vacation.

We would also like each guest to wear a red accessory. Yes, even you, Uncle Eugene! This will symbolize our love and add a splash of color to our celebration. Think of a tie, a bracelet, earrings or even red socks if you’re feeling bold!

The essential is that you feel comfortable and can fully enjoy this special day with us. And remember, the most important thing is to come with your smile and good humor!

Thank you for your understanding and for helping to make our wedding even more memorable!
